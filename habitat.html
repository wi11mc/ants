Ant Habitats

Ants are found on every continent except Antarctica. They inhabit a wide range of environments, including forests, grasslands, deserts, and urban areas. Some ant species prefer moist environments, while others thrive in dry climates.

Ant colonies can be found underground, in rotting wood, under rocks, or even inside plant stems. They create elaborate tunnels and chambers to house their queen, workers, and brood.
